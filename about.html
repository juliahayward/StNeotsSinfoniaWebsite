Our Concerts

Our concerts are usually held in St Mary's Church, Eaton Socon, on Saturday evenings at 7:30 PM

Rehearsals

Venue:The main rehearsal venue is Eynesbury Junior School, Montagu Street, Eynesbury

Time: 7:45 PM to 9:30 PM

When: Tuesday evenings during school term time.

Membership

Playing: Our membership runs from September to September and costs £80, or £27 per term (three terms).

Patron: Become a Patron of the society with a donation of £40 or more. This entitles you to two free tickets for all the concerts in that year

Sponsorship: Being a not-for-profit registered charity we are always grateful for sponsorship.

Governance

The orchestra is a Registered Charity, number 1115601.

Objects

“TO PROMOTE, IMPROVE, DEVELOP AND MAINTAIN PUBLIC EDUCATION IN, AND APPRECIATION OF, THE ART AND SCIENCE OF MUSIC IN ALL ITS ASPECTS BY THE PRESENTATION OF PUBLIC CONCERTS, AND BY SUCH OTHER WAYS AS THE SOCIETY THROUGH ITS COMMITTEE, SHALL DETERMINE FROM TIME TO TIME.”
