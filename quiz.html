20th Anniversary Quiz

Win two tickets to the Gala Concert on Saturday 7 November 7.30 – Eaton Socon Church plus a bottle of wine to be presented at the concert.

Answers with your name and address or telephone number by 31st October to Sinfonia Quiz, The Ferns, Berkley St., Eynesbury, St Neots, PE19 2NE or raggatt2@onetel.net

Answer the following questions. Good luck!

  1. How many conductors has the St Neots Sinfonia had since it was founded in 1989?
  2. Beethoven wrote nine symphonies – how many have Sinfonia performed?
  3. What is the age of the youngest member currently playing in the orchestra?
  4. Which concerto will be played at the 20th Anniversary Concert?
  5. St Neots has its own ‘Last Night of the Proms’ – who conducted the first Prom Concert which developed into the BBC Proms?
  6. How many of these occupations can be found in the orchestra: policeman, accountant, former bookmaker, environmentalist, doctor?
  7. How many commonly used orchestral instruments use a reed?
  8. How many strings are there on a violin?
  9. Britain came second in the Eurovision Song contest in 1989 – which country beat us?
  10. Who is the President of the St Neots Sinfonia? (clue: he is also the soloist who has played most concerti with the Sinfonia)?

Should there be a tie, the winner will be chosen from a draw of the entrants with the correct answers. The judges’ decision is final.
